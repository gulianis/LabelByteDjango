FAQ:

  1. What type of data can be labeled?
    Currently only images.

  2. What type of labels can be used?
    Bounding Boxes and Points.

  3. Where can I create an account?
    Either click register on the navigation bar or tap create account on the app

  4. Where can I upload data?
    Website: Once logged in click Label on the navigation bar.
    App: Once logged in press Upload Button in the top right corner. Places images you would like to upload in a folder and compress them into a zip file. Upload the zip file.

  5. How can I download label data?
    Website: Select Label on the navigation bar. Scroll down and select download button
    App: Once logged in press Options Button in the top left corner. Select download label data from pop up menu.

  6. Why is uploaded data deleted after 7 days?
    See question below.

  7. Why are there limits to data upload and accessibility?
    LabelByte is currently in beta. If LabelByte generates interest more functionality and accessibility will be added.
