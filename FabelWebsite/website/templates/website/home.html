Welcome to Fabel!

Fabel provides on the go data labeling for your machine learning projects.

Will soon be available for iphone.



What Fabel does:


Fabel allows you to label image data on your phone. It will soon be available for iphone. Once
you create an account, you can upload data to the cloud. Download the app and login on your phone with
the same account. You can access these photos and label them with bounding boxes or points. The coordinate data of these
labels in addition to a classification label for bounding boxes is downloadable as a txt file.


What an account has access to:

monthly upload limit file upload limit requests per uploaded image access time for uploaded file
